Administrator Features:
Admin Login: Secure login for administrators to access management
functionalities.

Customer Management: View and manage all registered customers.Update
specific customer details. Delete customer accounts if necessary.

Sushi Item Management: Add new sushi items to the menu, including
descriptions, prices, and availability. Edit details of existing menu items. Remove
sushi items from the menu as needed.

Order Management: View and manage all customer orders, ensuring timely
processing and updates to order statuses (e.g., "Pending," "Preparing,"
"Completed," "Cancelled").

Handle payments and other logistical details related to customer orders.

Profile Management: Administrators can update their profile information, such
as email and contact number.
