Customer Features:
Signup/Login: New customers must register to create an account.
Existing customers can log in to access their profiles and functionalities.

Profile Management: Customers can view and update their personal
information, such as contact details and address.

Sushi Menu Browsing: Customers can explore the available sushi items with
detailed descriptions and prices.

Order Placement: Customers can select sushi items, specify quantities, and
place orders directly through the platform.

Order History and Status Tracking: Customers can view their order history and
check the status of current orders.

Feedback Submission: Customers can provide feedback on their orders,
helping the shop improve its services.
